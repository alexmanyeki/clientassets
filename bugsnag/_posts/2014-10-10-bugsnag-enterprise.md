We’re excited to announce the launch of [Bugsnag Enterprise](https://www.bugsnag.com/pricing/), the on-premise version of Bugsnag that you can host on your own servers. Just like using [Bugsnag.com](https://www.bugsnag.com/), product teams using Bugsnag Enterprise can painlessly debug their software, saving them time to work on the projects that matter most.

![Bugsnag Enterprise](/img/posts/enterprise.png)

## Secure, on-premise solution
With Bugsnag Enterprise, companies with information security policies or regulatory concerns can take advantage of our fantastic, [cross-platform](https://docs.bugsnag.com/platforms/) crash monitoring service knowing their data will always be secure.

You can ship and maintain error-free technology while keeping control of all your proprietary information. Also, companies in industries like payments, finance, healthcare, and government, that have compliance needs can use Bugsnag while making sure their data never leaves their data center.

## Same great features
Bugsnag Enterprise has all the same amazing [features](https://www.bugsnag.com/product/) to help you increase your development team's productivity. With automatic [error alerts](https://www.bugsnag.com/integrations/), and deep diagnostic information, you'll spend a fraction of the time finding and fixing bugs.

Bugsnag provides actionable error information—the answers developers need to efficiently deal with bugs and move on. It'll change the way you deal with bug fixing, saving you time to build and release the features that'll make your customers happy.
